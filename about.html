About My Pack

I have two fantastic - and very different - dogs. Although they both love meeting new friends and helping me with demonstration in classes, they have each challenged me in different ways and taught me things very different things about dogs.

My two-year-old Australian shepherd has a sweet, sensitive personality with a knack for bringing anyone into the pack in short order, while my 8-year-old terrier has that classic, independent terrier spirit with a goofy, playful side and a penchant for snuggling everyone he meets.
