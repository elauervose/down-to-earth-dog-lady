Private Lessons

Whether you need to work on new skill, refresh an old one, or solve a behavior problem, private lessons are the perfect place to work with me and your dog in a highly focused and personalized way.

I can meet you downtown, at your house, or at a location you choose. We'll start with a consultation and from that, I'll create a personalized training plan for you and your dog. Then we'll work together to meet your training goals!

Contact me directly to schedule a private lesson.

Consultation:
I like to start with a consultation to get to know you and your dog. We'll go over my training methods and begin to construct a training plan together!

Drop-In Classes

Do you need a refresh on some of your dog's commands? Has your dog recently developed a naughty behavior that you don't understand and need help with? You're probably not alone!

Drop-in classes are the perfect place to work on problems in a small group setting where you can get feed-back and moral support from other dog owners

Drop-In lessons are 45 minutes in length and are typically offered on a weekly basis.

For drop-in dates and times, see the class descriptions & calendar below.
If no drop-in classes are currently being offered, email me to let me know you are interested.

Pricing:

  • Single drop-in lesson: $25
  • Package of 3 drop-in lessons: $60
    (Drop-in packages expire 3 months after date of purchase)
Multi-Week Classes

These classes are goal driven and designed to build on each other. We'll start with basics and work toward more advanced skills.

Each week, you'll get a homework assignment for you and your dog to work on together. The following week, we'll review the assignment and I'll give feedback on your progress.

Class topics vary widely from basic manners to advanced competitive obedience (Rally-O).

Pricing varies by class; typically costs range from $80-$120 for 4 - 6 week long classes.

For details, see class descriptions & calendar below.
If you don't see a class you want, or don't see dates and times for a class listed, email me to let me know you are interested.
